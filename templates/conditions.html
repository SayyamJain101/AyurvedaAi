üß† Digestive Issues

IBS & Constipation

Cause (Ayurvedic view): Vata imbalance
Remedies: Triphala, warm oil massage, ginger tea
Diet: Warm, oily, grounding foods
Lifestyle: Regular meal times, adequate hydration
Herbs: Fennel, cumin, coriander in meals

Bloating & Gas

Cause: Weak agni or Vata imbalance
Remedies: Ginger before meals, digestive spices
Diet: Avoid cold water during meals
Herbs: Asafetida (hing), fennel seeds
Practices: Slow eating, thorough chewing

Acid Reflux & Heartburn

Cause: Pitta imbalance
Remedies: Slippery elm, licorice root, brahmi
Diet: Cool, non-spicy, sweet foods
Avoid: Spicy, oily, acidic foods
Practices: Eat 3 hours before sleep, cool drinks

Insomnia

Cause: Vata or Pitta imbalance
Remedies: Warm milk with ashwagandha, chamomile tea
Herbs: Valerian, passionflower, brahmi
Practices: Abhyanga (oil massage), meditation
Lifestyle: Sleep by 10 PM, consistent routine

Anxiety & Restlessness

Cause: Vata imbalance
Remedies: Grounding practices, warm oils
Herbs: Ashwagandha, brahmi, jatamansi
Techniques: Pranayama (slow breathing), yoga
Foods: Dates, ghee, sesame oil, warm soups

Stress & Burnout

Cause: All doshas, excessive stimulation
Remedies: Adaptogenic herbs, rest
Herbs: Ashwagandha, shatavari, brahmi
Practices: Meditation, yoga, nature time
Lifestyle: Reduce stimulation, adequate sleep
