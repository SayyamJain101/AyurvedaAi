üß† Digestive Health & Agni (‡§Ö‡§ó‡•ç‡§®‡§ø)

Understanding Agni

\"Agni\" (digestive fire) is the foundation of good health. Strong agni ensures proper digestion, nutrient absorption, and elimination of waste. Weak agni leads to \"ama\" (toxic accumulation), causing disease. Ayurvedic principle: \"Agnimantrah param rakshanam\" - \"A strong digestive fire is the best protection.\"

Improving Digestion

Ginger tea: Ignites digestive fire
Bitter herbs: Stimulate bile production
Warm meals: Easier to digest
Spices: Turmeric, cumin, coriander enhance digestion
Timing: Eat at consistent times

Foods for Each Dosha

Vata: Warm, oily, grounding foods
Pitta: Cool, sweet, bitter foods
Kapha: Light, dry, warming foods
Eating according to your dosha naturally supports healthy digestion and balanced energy.

üò¥ Sleep & Rest Quality

Ayurvedic Sleep Wisdom

Sleep is one of three pillars of health. Quality sleep rejuvenates all tissues, processes emotions, and consolidates memory. Ayurveda provides proven methods for better sleep naturally.

Enhance Sleep Naturally

Warm milk: With ashwagandha or nutmeg at bedtime
Oil massage: Abhyanga before sleep
Early sleep: Before 10 PM (Kapha time)
No screens: 1 hour before bed
Consistent routine: Sleep and wake at same times

Sleep by Dosha

Vata insomnia: Root vegetables, warm oils
Pitta sleep issues: Cooling herbs like brahmi
Kapha oversleeping: Stimulating practices, lighter diet
Personalized approaches based on your constitution work best.
